Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my child's class schedule?

You can request a class change through the Class Management page. Select the current class and desired new class, provide a reason, and submit the request. Changes typically take 3-5 business days to process and may incur a $50 administrative fee.

What is the refund policy for class cancellations?

Refunds are prorated based on the number of classes attended. A 30-day notice is required for cancellations. Cancellation fees may apply based on your enrollment contract terms. Contact our billing department for specific calculations.

How do I put a class on hold?

Classes can be held for up to 3 months with a $25/month administrative fee. Use the Class Management page to submit a hold request. You'll retain your spot in the class and can resume at any time during the hold period.

How can I track my child's progress?

Progress is tracked through our Learning Pathway page, which shows real-time updates on assignments, test scores, and overall performance. You'll also receive weekly progress reports via email and push notifications for important updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. Auto-pay discounts are available for monthly subscriptions. Payment is due by the 1st of each month.
